Phase diff of incoming tilted beam on local gaussian for inf. half-plane PD¶

\begin{equation} \Delta \phi(\alpha) = 2\arctan (\frac{\sqrt{2 \pi} w_{0} \alpha}{\lambda})\end{equation}\begin{align} \frac{d \Delta \phi}{d \alpha} =& 2\frac{1}{1+(\frac{\sqrt{2 \pi} w_{0} \alpha}{\lambda})^2}\frac{\sqrt{2 \pi} w_{0}}{\lambda} \\=& 2\frac{\sqrt{2 \pi}w_0 \lambda}{\lambda^2+2 \pi w_0^2 \alpha^2} \end{align}

Alex's result

\begin{equation} \Phi_{dif} = \arctan \left[ \text{erfi}(\frac{kw(z) \sin \alpha}{2 \sqrt{2}}) \right] \end{equation}
